We are Hack//Ridge.

On Saturday March 4, join the most innovative people you'll ever meet for 24 exhilerating hours of caffinated hacking in Park Ridge, IL. We bring together high school students to build the coolest hardware and software projects in Chicago. Hack//Ridge is the place for you to express your creativity, ingenuity, and determination to create anything you want to.
